# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026

## 1. Introduction

CleanCalc ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the CleanCalc mobile and web application.

## 2. Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

CleanCalc is a frontend-only application. All data processing and storage occurs locally on your device.

## 3. Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device:

- **Service Types**: Custom cleaning service offerings and base prices
- **Pricing Rules**: Your configured pricing factors and fees
- **Quotes**: Generated quotes with property details and calculations
- **Settings**: App preferences (theme, language, currency, units)
- **Templates**: Saved quote templates for reuse

## 4. How Local Data is Used

Local data is used solely to:

- Perform pricing calculations
- Save your preferences and settings
- Maintain quote history
- Enable template reuse

## 5. Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with third parties** because:

- All data remains on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No analytics or tracking services are used
- No advertising networks have access to your data

## 6. PDF Export and Sharing

When you export or share a quote as PDF:

- The PDF is generated locally on your device
- You control where and how the PDF is shared
- We do not receive copies of exported PDFs
- Sharing occurs through your device's native share functionality

## 7. Data Security

Your data security depends on:

- Your device's security settings
- Your device's operating system protections
- Physical access controls to your device

We recommend:

- Using device encryption
- Setting a strong device passcode
- Keeping your operating system updated

## 8. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you:

- Manually delete it through the App
- Clear the App's data through device settings
- Uninstall the App

## 9. Children's Privacy

CleanCalc is intended for business use by cleaning professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 10. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: View all data within the App
- **Modify**: Edit or update any stored information
- **Delete**: Remove individual quotes or clear all data
- **Export**: Generate PDFs of your quotes

## 11. Third-Party Services

CleanCalc does not integrate with third-party services that collect data. All functionality is self-contained.

## 12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 13. Platform-Specific Information

### Web Version
- Data stored in browser local storage
- Clearing browser data will delete App data
- No cookies are used for tracking

### Mobile Version (iOS/Android)
- Data stored in app-specific storage
- Accessible only by the App
- Removed when App is uninstalled

### Desktop Version (macOS/Windows)
- Data stored in application documents directory
- Protected by operating system permissions
